This plugin adds a mailing list subscribe form to your blog that automatically syncs with Direct Mail on your Mac

== Description ==

If you use [Direct Mail for Mac](https://directmailmac.com), this widget places a small subscribe form on your blog that lets visitors quickly and easily subscribe to your mailing list. Before using this plugin, be sure to [set up your subscribe form](https://directmailmac.com/support/article/225) in Direct Mail.

Email and live chat support is available at [our support site](https://directmailmac.com/support).

== Installation ==

1. Upload `DirectMailSubscribeForm.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Use the 'Appearance > Widgets' menu in WordPress to place the widget on your blog.
